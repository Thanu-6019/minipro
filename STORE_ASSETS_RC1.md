## 2. Privacy Policy Requirements

*Note: This is a summary of requirements. A full legal document must be drafted by a legal professional and hosted at a public URL.*

**Core Requirements for Health Apps:**
1.  **Data Collection Disclosure:** Explicitly state that we collect:
    *   **Personal Information:** Name, email, profile picture.
    *   **Health/Medical Data:** Medication names, dosages, schedules, blood pressure, blood sugar, heart oxygen, weight, BMI, and adherence logs.
    *   **Usage Data:** App interaction patterns for performance optimization.
2.  **Purpose of Collection:** Clearly explain that data is used for:
    *   Providing core app functionality (reminders, tracking).
    *   Generating personalized health insights and trends.
    *   Improving AI extraction accuracy (OCR).
3.  **Data Sharing & Third Parties:**
    *   Disclose use of **Supabase** for secure data storage and authentication.
    *   Disclose use of **AI/OCR services** (e.g., Grok, OpenOCR) for prescription processing.
    *   Disclose use of **OpenFDA** for medication information.
    *   *Crucial:* State that health data is **not sold to third parties** or advertisers.
4.  **User Rights (GDPR/CCPA):**
    *   Right to access, correct, and delete personal/health data.
    *   Right to data portability.
    *   Right to withdraw consent at any time.
5.  **Data Security:** Describe the use of encryption (AES-256) and secure storage (Expo SecureStore) to protect data.
